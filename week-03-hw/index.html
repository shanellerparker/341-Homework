Broken

Shanelle Parker

          I see my reflection in a shattered mirror,

          Sorrow scarred on my flesh,

          Hatred embedded within,

          Yet, love is in my heart,

          Still, fear of losing causes me to ache,

          Tears water, warming my cheek,

          I'm broken into so many jagged pieces,

          Will I ever be beautiful?
