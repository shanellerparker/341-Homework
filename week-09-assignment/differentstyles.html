Different Beading Styles:



There are many different styles of Native American beadwork. Each with their own positives and negitives. This interpretation depends on your own personal preference and cultural meaning behind the particular stitch. These different styles include but are not limited to what are listed.


Styles:

  • Peyote
  • Loom
  • Brick Stitch
  • Flat Peyote
  • Tubular Peyote
  • Lazy Stitch
  • Applique
  • Flat Stitch
  • Pyrimad Edge
  • Rope Stitch
  • Diagonal Edge
  • One Bead Edge
