Native American Beadwork



Native American beadwork is a tradition that is usually passed down within a Native American family. It is considered an art.It is accomplished with the use of thread, needles, stable foundation material. Several other components in modern beadwork include seed beads, rhinestone band, and crystals. It is more than a craft it is a piece of Native American culture.
